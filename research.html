Research interests

  • Sketching-based optimization solvers: Scale standard optimization solvers using sketching-based preconditioners (aka random projections).
  • Neural networks: Characterize the training landscape of neural networks through a convex optimization perspective.
  • Reinforcement and imitation learning: Improve the performance of autonomous agents in safety-critical contexts using risk-sensitive optimization formulations.
