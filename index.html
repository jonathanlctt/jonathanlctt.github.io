Jonathan Lacotte

I am a fifth-year PhD student in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Stanford University where I am fortunate to be advised by Mert Pilanci. I obtained a B.S. and M.S. at the Ecole Polytechnique, and I completed the Part III of the Mathematical Tripos at Cambridge University. I also worked at UC Berkeley as a visiting student researcher with Laurent El Ghaoui, and interned at the Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency, Facebook AI Research and G-Research.

My research interests are broadly in convex optimization and deep learning. Currently, I focus on understanding the optimization landscape of neural networks through a convex perspective and on improving the speed and memory of first-order methods using sketching tools. Previously, I developed reinforcement and imitation learning algorithms for safety-critical applications.
